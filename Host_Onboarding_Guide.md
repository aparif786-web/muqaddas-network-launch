Here are concise guidelines for creating the **Host Onboarding Guide** and **Coin Spending Education** documents for your Muqaddas Network. These documents will help new users understand how to engage with the platform effectively.

### **1. Host_Onboarding_Guide.md**

```markdown
# Host Onboarding Guide

**Effective Date:** [Insert date]

## Introduction
Welcome to the Muqaddas Network! This guide will help you become a successful host, whether you are a Normal Host or a Star Host, and understand our 7-day policy.

---

## Becoming a Host

### 1. Normal Host
- **Eligibility**: Must be 18 years or older.
- **Requirements**:
  - Complete the application form.
  - Provide valid identification.
  - Attend the onboarding webinar.

### 2. Star Host
- **Eligibility**: Proven track record as a successful Normal Host.
- **Requirements**:
  - Minimum of 100 completed sessions.
  - Positive feedback from at least 80% of participants.
  - Application for Star Host status.

### Benefits of Being a Host
- Earn stars for every session.
- Access to exclusive events and resources.
- Enhanced visibility and credibility on the platform.

---

## 7-Day Policy
- **Day 1**: Complete onboarding and setup your profile.
- **Day 2-3**: Conduct at least one session each day.
- **Day 4**: Gather participant feedback.
- **Day 5**: Review your performance metrics.
- **Day 6**: Make necessary adjustments based on feedback.
- **Day 7**: Submit your report to become a Star Host, if eligible.

---

## Conclusion
Thank you for joining us on this mission! Together, we can make a real difference in the lives of those in need.

For any questions, please contact our support team.

**Version**: 1.0  
**Last Updated**: [Insert date]
```
