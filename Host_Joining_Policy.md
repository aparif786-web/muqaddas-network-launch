## Eligibility Requirements

To become a host on Muqaddas Network, you must meet the following criteria:

1. **Age Requirement**: You must be at least 18 years old to register as a host.
2. **Identity Verification**: All hosts must provide valid identification (e.g., government-issued ID, passport) for verification purposes.
3. **Account in Good Standing**: Your account must not have any outstanding violations or penalties.
