## 1. Event Structure

### 1.1 Types of Events
- **Single Elimination**: Participants are eliminated after a single loss.
- **Double Elimination**: Participants have a second chance after one loss.
- **Round Robin**: Each participant competes against all others.

### 1.2 Duration
- Each battle will last for a predefined time (e.g., **5 minutes**) or until a winner is determined.

## 3. Scoring and Rewards

### 3.1 Scoring System
- Points are awarded based on performance during battles:
  - **Win**: 100 points
  - **Loss**: 50 points
  - **Participation**: 10 points
  
### 3.2 Rewards
- **Cash Prizes**: Winners will receive cash rewards based on their ranking.
- **Bonus Points**: Additional points will be awarded for participation and achievements.
- **Leaderboard Recognition**: Top performers will be displayed on the leaderboard.
